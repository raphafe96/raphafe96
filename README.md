### 🔬 What I Do

**Vibrational Spectroscopy** – Anharmonic vibrational structure calculations using VSCF (Vibrational Self-Consistent Field) and VCI (Vibrational Configuration Interaction) methods.

I develop **ViBra**, an open-source toolkit for accurate vibrational spectra prediction. The code implements selected and symmetry-adapted VCI approaches, bridging quantum chemical calculations with spectroscopy.

**Optoelectronic Properties** – Electronic structure methods (DFT, ab initio), structure-property relationships, and prediction of polarizabilities and dipole moments for molecular crystals and biomolecules.

I develop and maintain **GruPol** (and GFF-CrysPol), a computational suite for molecular dynamics simulations and a database of building-block optoelectronic properties for proteins and molecular crystals. The approach enables rapid prediction of electric and electrostatic properties of macromolecules.
